About the Site

E-watch is dedicated to your e-sports needs, providing the best coverage when you need it, no matter what you watch. E-watch allows you to have all the best esports at your fingertips. With E-watch, you can learn about new up and coming esports, and, maybe even play them one day. Our site will fit your needs, no matter the e-sport. This Website:

  • Allows users to get e-sports feed the way they want it.
  • Allows for users to see any esports content in one place.
  • Allows for learning on esports topics.
  • Gives the user options in their watching experience.
